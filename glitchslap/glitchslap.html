"Slap the bit out of 'em"

Remember playing games with your friends as a kid? Sitting around the TV for hours, kicking each others butts?? I want to bring that back. Glitch Slap wants to bring that back! Your friend's butts will never be the same.

Glitch Slap is a local multiplayer party game that will have both co-op and vs. modes. You slap each other around competing in tons of different arenas or work together to conquer the co-op arenas. Glitch Slap is being developed for the current generation of consoles and PC with a tentative release date late in 2017.
