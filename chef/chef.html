Anthony Tesija

Private chef in Lansing Michigan using seasonal ingredients

AnTesija@gmail.com

I can cook for one to eight people. I've been cooking for ten years now ever since my parents had me start when I was young. I'm continuously trying to improve by reading about cooking, watching other chefs, and practicing myself. I source all my ingredients locally whenever possible so my dishes will vary based on the season.

🌱☀️🍂❄️ My seasonal ingredients come from the Holt farmer's market right down the street from me.
🐓🥚 My chicken comes from Otto's Chicken. They have the best tasting antibiotic and hormone free chicken in Michigan.
🐟🦐🐙🦀 My seafood comes from Oceanside Seafood. In my opinion they import the highest quality seafood in Michigan.
🐄🥓🍖🍔 My meat comes from Mert's Specialty Meats.
🍆🍎🧀 Everything else I get from Fresh Thyme, a local Lansing market with an emphasis on local, organic products.

If you'd like to book me for a private dinner or small gathering please email me at AnTesija@gmail.com with the date and time and I'll get back to you as soon as I see it! The cost is $50 per person plus the cost of ingredients (estimated between $5 and $20 per person). Let me know of any food restrictions or allergies so I can plan around them. I also take reccomendations on what you'd like to eat. If you're feeling seafood, or something citrus-y, or something roasted, or want a specific dish just ask! You can browse through my Instagram to find things I've cooked before and ask for any of them. Thanks!
