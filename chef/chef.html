I source all my ingredients locally whenever possible so my dishes will vary based on the season
Seafood https://www.oceansideseafood.com/ Chicken http://www.ottoschicken.com/ Seasonal ingredients https://holtfarmersmarket.org/ Everything else https://www.freshthyme.com/

I can cook for one to eight people. My parents had me start cooking when I was young and I continued on always trying to improve. I've been cooking for ten years now.

If you'd like to book me for a private dinner or small gathering please email me at AnTesija@gmail.com with the date and time and I'll get back to you as soon as I see it! Let me know of any food restrictions or allergies so I can plan around them. Thanks!
