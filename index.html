Jettison

2015 - Android and iOS

A meteor has heavily damaged the factory you work in leaving it ready to explode at any time. Your space suit has been damaged in the process and is stuck in run mode. Use your jetpack to navigate through 30 daring rooms and make it to the escape pod!

Jettison is a puzzle platformer that features 30 hand crafted levels designed to test your skills. The game is easy to learn and play and only requires a single press to fire your jetpack. You only have a small amount of fuel that will recharge when while you are touching the ground. The challenge comes with knowing when to fire your jetpack and when to release it. Jettison is $1.99 on both Android and iOS and will be available March 20th.

Galactic Planet Wrangler

2014 - Web

Do you like cowboys and space? Well it turns out that mixing the two was an absolutely awful idea, but here's a game about both anyways.

In Galactic Planet Wrangler your task is to round up as many planets as possible before crashing into the sun. Your controls consist of riding your asteroid as you barrel towards your impending doom or yelling YEEHAW! because you feel like it. Left Click blasts your asteroid in the direction clicked, Right Click yells YEEHAW! Use your amazing abilities to collect as many planets and asteroids as you can before sacrificing them to the sun. Oh, and mouse wheel zooms. Galactic Planet Wrangler was made for Ludum Dare 30

Dizzy's World

2014 - Web

Dizzy's World is a puzzle platformer where you guide Dizzy through dangerous worlds to his home. Dizzy is pretty old so he can't jump like his famous platforming counterparts, but he does have vertigo and the world seems to shift around him. Use his nausiating power to bring him home!

Dizzy's world was made for GBJam3 and recieved 84th out of 248 submissions.

Sneak Thief

2011 - PC and Linux

Make your way through this puzzle game collecting each level's gems and avoiding all the traps. There are four items that can be found in each level that will help you on your way. Sneak Thief comes with 50 levels and a level editor so you can make and share levels with your friends.

Blender

2011 - PC and Linux

Combine colored orbs to open doors in this perilous puzzle maze. Each colored orb opens it's corresponding colored door. The rainbow orbs allow you to combine your current colors to unlock new paths.

This game was made for Wolverine Soft's 48 hour game competition and won 3rd place out of 16 entries. The theme was "combine". Wolverine Soft is University of Michigan's game development club.

Lumberjack Zed Attack

2010 - PC and Linux

An arcade survival game about a burly lumberjack and his axe.

Zombies spawn from all corners of the screen and they can smell you. Use your trusty axe to cut down trees and lay traps using the wood. Rescue survivors along the way for bonus points! The zombies get faster over time so watch out!
