Lumberjack Zed Attack

An arcade survival game about a burly lumberjack and his axe.

Zombies spawn from all corners of the screen and they can smell you. Use your trusty axe to cut down trees and lay traps using the wood. Rescue survivors along the way for bonus points! The zombies get faster over time so watch out!
