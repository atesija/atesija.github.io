Jettison is a puzzle platformer that features 30 levels designed to test your skills. You play as an astronaut escaping an exploding factory on the moon. The factory you're escaping has deadly traps including acid and lasers. The game is easy to learn and play, only requiring a single press to fire your jetpack. You have a small amount of fuel that will recharge when you are touching the ground. The challenge comes with maneuvering through treacherous traps while trying conserving fuel. Each level has a key and collecting it will open the door to the next level. A challenge mode featuring impossibly hard levels and a time trial mode are both planned to be released within two months of initial release.

Jettison will be available on iOS and Android phones and tablets in March for $1.99. The game will be available in all countries able to purchase apps through those stores.
